Common questions

Before you book.

Do you need my garage, hose, or outlet?

No. The rig carries its own water, power, and extraction equipment. A regular driveway, carport, or parking space is enough. If you live in an apartment without a dedicated spot, street parking or a friend's driveway works just as well.

How long does a full interior take?

Most cars are done in 3-5 hours. SUVs, minivans, and heavy mold or water jobs can stretch to a full day. You don't need to be there the whole time - leave us keys or a lock code and go about your day.

Can you actually get smoke smell out?

Yes. Our multi-step process pulls 90-95% of trapped odors - smoke, pet, food, or the one you can't place. Heavy smoke on a smoker's daily driver might need two visits. We tell you up front either way.

Do I need to be home while you work?

Not at all. Plenty of customers leave keys and a lock code, then come home to a fresh car. Pay by Zelle, Venmo, or cash when you're happy with the result.

Which neighborhoods do you serve?

The full Sacramento metro - Downtown, Midtown, Arden-Arcade, Natomas, Elk Grove, Folsom, Rancho Cordova, Carmichael, Citrus Heights, and everything between. Plus Roseville and Rocklin, West Sac, Fairfield, and Vacaville. Not sure? Text your ZIP.

What does it cost?

Prices vary by vehicle size and interior condition. The fastest way to get a number is to text 2-3 photos of the inside plus what you're trying to solve (odor, spill, general clean, mold). Same-day quote, no obligation.

What if I just spilled something big?

Call immediately - (916) 707-7455. For water, coffee, or anything that's soaking in, we try to come same-day or next-day. The faster we extract, the lower your risk of mold and permanent damage.
